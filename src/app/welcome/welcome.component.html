Welcome to Enable Scaler

eNable Scaler is a web site that lets you create 3D printable prosthetics custom to your measurements and selected options. The prosthetics are intended for use by people with no hand. The 3D printable model used is called the Gripper Thumb.


The steps are:
  1. Enter Measurements
  2. Deliver files that can be printed on a 3D printer.
Are you ready to enter your Measurements?

Click the "Start" button after accepting the terms to begin!
