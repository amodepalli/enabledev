Instructions

If you would like to download a small, medium, or large hand please select one of the options below and press download. However, if you would like to get a more accurate hand based on your non amputated hand, skip this part, and continue to follow the rest of the instructions.





Please select the units for measurment first. Then enter your height, and weight and press the download button to retrieve the necessary STL files that are required to print the prosthetic hand.

Palm length and middle finger length are optional measurements, these will give you a more accurate print. If you would like to enter these measurements, take a look at the following photos to ensure that you are measuring correctly.
